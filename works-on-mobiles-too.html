Works on mobiles too!07 Jul 2013

Since day one I've been planning support for modern smartphones and here is a first working version:

This is an actual screenshot from my Nexus 4, currently left half of the screen controls moving and the right one - shooting. On the screenshot above you can see orange rings as a response to my touch input. I've adapted brilliantly simple virtualjoystick.js lib to support touch input.

Oh and archers are no longer bald!
